# Architecture Decision Record

ADR-001: One Case, One Database.
ADR-002: Reports are outputs, not the investigation.
ADR-003: Workspaces follow the investigation workflow.
ADR-004: People and Interviews are separate.
ADR-005: Fire personnel are People with roles.
ADR-006: Photos attach to objects; standalone photo workspace only if photo log is needed.
ADR-007: Fire patterns are documented inside the relevant examination area.
ADR-008: Area of Origin is its own workspace.
ADR-009: Ignition sources are dynamic cards/matrix, not pre-populated.
ADR-010: No automatic conclusions by the software.
ADR-011: No automatic timestamps for every observation.
